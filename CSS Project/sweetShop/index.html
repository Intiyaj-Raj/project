About Us

Bakers use special tools like ovens, mixers, and baking sheets. They also use recipes, some of which may have been passed down through generations. There are different types of bakeries including retail bakeries, where we can walk in and buy one or two items, and wholesale bakeries, which make large quantities of baked goods for stores and restaurants.
